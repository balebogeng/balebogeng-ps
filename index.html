About us

We are a primary school situated Mamelodi, Pretoria dedicated to enthusiastically engaging our students in their learning, supported by well-trained and caring teachers who work to ensure the best opportunities for every child.

Vision

Ensure every student at Balebogeng Primary School does well at school and leave the institution with values, knowledge, skills and qualifications that will give him/her the best chance of success in adult life.

Mission

To ensure quality learning and teaching takes place in the classroom everyday.
