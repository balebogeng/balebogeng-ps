About us

We are a primary school situated in Mamelodi, Pretoria, dedicated to enthusiastically engaging our students in their learning, supported by well-trained and caring staff who work to ensure the best opportunities for every student.

Vision

Our vision is to ensure that every student performs well academically and leaves the institution with values, knowledge, skills and qualifications that will give him or her the best chance of success in future.

Mission

We strive to ensure that quality learning and teaching takes place our classrooms everyday so that every student may have the opportunity to make the most of his or her education.
